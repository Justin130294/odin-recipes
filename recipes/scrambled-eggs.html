Scrambled eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs are a tasty and healthy breakfast meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon mayonnaise or salad dressing
  • 1 teaspoon water (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon margarine or butter
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a cup or small bowl, whisk together the eggs, mayonnaise and water using a fork.
  2. Melt margarine in a skillet over low heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs, and stir constantly as they cook.
  4. Remove the eggs to a plate when they are set, but still moist. Do not over cook.
